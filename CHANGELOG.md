# Changelog

## Unreleased

Demo and adapter fixes after first live use: the layer repaints on move and zoom events instead of snapping at moveend, resets the trail when the clock runs backward (the looping pass wiped it after the first orbit), skips clock-driven repaints for treatments that ignore the clock, and trackStrip gains a whiskbroom-style scan option so the mechanism treatment has instrument detail to reveal when zoomed in; the demo defaults to the mechanism treatment, making the envelope-to-mechanism LOD hybrid the front-door experience.

Demo iteration series (post slice 6, field-tested live): atlas-faithful treatments under the atlas names, the dark ground and two more basemaps, per-instrument layers across nine satellites and twelve instruments, live plan execution with tasked windows and per-segment state re-emission, the LOD scan-mechanism hybrid, cache-stamped atomic deploys, and the field fixes (trail smear and loop reset, header layout, footprint scale under integer-rounding projectors, now-marker sync and expiry, resilient startup). Engine and adapter API added along the way: trackStrip with scan, offset ribbon, and bilateral postures, withStateRule, AcquisitionLayer.updateStates, TREATMENT_LABELS, paintGuide.

Phase 1 sixth slice: the demo-leaflet app and the provider bridge. trackStrip in core turns one target's StateBatch block into a strip by spherical footprint geometry (nadir from the position direction, cross-track from the orbit normal, great-circle edge and bead offsets, body radius supplied by the caller). The demo propagates fresh ISS and ICESat-2 TLEs in a worker-hosted Sgp4Provider through the port seam, builds a pushbroom envelope and a bead chain, and paints them with AcquisitionLayer over OpenStreetMap tiles with treatment, speed, and pause controls; the clock runs from each element epoch so the coverage fence always holds, and prefers-reduced-motion starts the page paused on one rendered pass. The docs pipeline bundles the demo with esbuild and ships it on Pages at /demo/ beside the static atlas.

Phase 1 fourth slice: the argelander-leaflet adapter. AcquisitionLayer paints strips into a canvas overlay through pure headless-tested modules: the six treatments as styling policies with the atlas state-hue palette and dash-pattern instrument textures (explicit overrides only), LOD by projected swath width with sparse geometries never ribboned, a connection rule that refuses to interpolate across time gaps, burst seams, or frame chains, per-quad antimeridian unwrapping with world copies, CRS delegation through the host projector, and the exponential trail-decay canvas with pausable speed-scaled animation. Leaflet becomes a peer dependency; the 21-family fixture corpus drives the painter tests.

Phase 1 third slice: samplers for the eight radar and scatterometer families. Stripmap (side-looking offset ribbon on Venus), ScanSAR/TOPS (burst-cycled sub-swaths with seams as time gaps), spotlight (pinned patch dwells, silent between), SweepSAR-DBF (one flooded swath with receive-beam markers), bistatic formation (baseline subs weaving the helix), fan-beam scatterometer (azimuth look cycling), pencil-beam scatterometer (two spinning bead beams, no nadir gap), and bilateral swath (one side of the gap plus the nadir altimeter chain on the distance clock), each with a code-owned scene, a model fixture round-trip test with mechanism assertions sourced from model constants, and its fixture regenerated as a sampler anchor.

Phase 1 second slice: samplers for the six along-track families. Whiskbroom (mirror sweep with bowtie footprints), framing (keystone frame outlines on the exposure clock), push-frame (lunar swath with filter-band markers), multi-angle (station sub-swaths with per-station looks), and profiler (zero-width bead rows on the distance clock) join the Phase 0 pushbroom anchor, each with a frozen atlas-tile scene and a model fixture round-trip test; the five worked-example strip fixtures retire in favor of regenerated sampler anchors.

Phase 1 first slice: ADR-0008 and the argelander-providers package with the near-earth SGP4 provider (from-source, Spacetrack Report #3 verification corpus, rendering-grade GMST earth-fixed frames, TLE coverage fence), the pre-sampled provider (Hermite state interpolation, slerped orientation, CSV ingestion), and worker port marshalling with transferable batches and structured refusal round-trip.

Seed scaffold: specs (draft), ADRs 0001-0006, phase goals, core types and validation with pushbroom fixture, atlas as day-one demo, CI with Pages deploy, style gate.
