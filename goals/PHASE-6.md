# PHASE-6: The model corrections

Subject: implement the two model corrections the platform-and-payload study derived and ADRs 0015 and 0016 accepted. The instrument catalog stores a swath baked at a nominal altitude and carries the real optics in a prose note, and the engine points every platform from its velocity, which collapses for the atmospheric and ballistic classes; this phase makes the intrinsic optics the model and makes measured attitude first-class, so the catalog carries queryable collecting geometry and a sounding rocket or a balloon renders on its real pointing rather than a confidently wrong velocity-derived one. Governed by ADR-0016 (the optics-first instrument model), ADR-0015 (the attitude epistemic envelope), and ADR-0014 (the frame boundary). Suborbital and balloon are a confirmed delivery target.

Deliver: intrinsic collecting-geometry parameters and a structured provenance block on the instrument model, riding the optional-parameter extension point so the frozen envelope and the required-parameter table do not move, a JSON instrument-model loader and a catalog manifest in argelander-core (the interchange contract AGE-18 has lacked), and the twenty-five catalog models migrated to carry the optics and the structured provenance with the baked parameter kept through the transition and the samplers preferring the optics; a rendering-grade spherical altitude-parametric optics-to-geometry derivation in argelander-core, the swath and footprint and near and far ground range computed from the optics and the per-segment platform altitude through the analytic sphere intersection of ADR-0014 within the rendering-grade budget of ADR-0018, so a swath is correct along an eccentric orbit or an airborne climb rather than frozen at one altitude; the attitude epistemic envelope on SPEC-PROVIDER as additive facets, an attitude source and quality tag, an attitude coverage window and a refusal symmetric to state coverage, and an angular rate, verified against the owned Bessel orientation contract and updated there if the subset requires it, together with the sampler path that consumes measured attitude where it is real, marks a segment assumed where the pointing is synthesized, and refuses or plans where attitude is unavailable, with ADR-0012 demoted to the fallback for the assumed-nadir orbital path; and the documentation reframe, now unblocked, leading with the source-agnostic seam and carrying honest suborbital, balloon, and planetary examples on measured or explicitly-assumed pointing. Out of scope and deliberately deferred: the composable-primitive taxonomy of ADR-0016 rule 6, which stays a later SPEC-INSTRUMENT-MODEL v2 concern.

Exit: a catalog model carries its intrinsic optics and a structured provenance block and loads through the manifest; one instrument derives two different swaths at two platform altitudes through the spherical derivation, and the atlas conformance replay still matches its committed fixtures within tolerance; an orientation result carries a source tag, a coverage window, and an angular rate, and refuses epochs outside the attitude window with a structured refusal; a sampler renders a sounding-rocket or a balloon footprint on measured attitude, painted committed only where the attitude is real; the frozen strip schema, the frozen required-parameter table, and the twenty-one conformance anchors are unmoved; and the charter holds, argelander-core still ships zero runtime dependencies, no path introduces propagation, orbit determination, frames math beyond the analytic sphere, or an embedded CSPICE. The parts that touch a frozen contract, the SPEC-INSTRUMENT-MODEL revision that eventually promotes the optics to required and the SPEC-PROVIDER revision that adds the attitude facets, land with their spec change in the same commit.
